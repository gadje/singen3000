Our Story

A Voice United Across Generations

Singen3000 was born from a shared belief: that choral music, rooted in centuries of tradition, still has something urgent and alive to say today. We perform the great classical repertoire — Bach, Brahms, Pärt — while remaining open to the unexpected.

Our singers come from all walks of life, bound by a love of harmony and a commitment to excellence. Rehearsals are rigorous, joyful, and always worth it.
